Students from three to fifteen years old can attend the small,
independent, secular School. We strive to nurture each student's
unique talents and cherish the whole child. We support independ-
ence, innovation, and critical thinking. Every child's social, emotional,
physical, and intellectual needs are respected. Our goal is to raise
courageous, moral, and inquisitive individuals who make meaningful
contributions to their local and international societies.
